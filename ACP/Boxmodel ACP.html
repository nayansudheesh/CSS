Floats

Float property is used for formatting and posistioning of images. Float right means it is only the right side of the screeen , float left means it is on the left side of the screen , even if you change screen size , it will move towards the right/left based on its assigned property

In this example the images float to left and right , and they wrap around the text if you reduce screen size.
